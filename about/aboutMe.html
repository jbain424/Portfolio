About Me

As a multi-disciplined artist by trade, I was fortunate to have my creative lens shaped by parents and mentors at an early age.  Those initial lessons were a huge influence in attaining my Graphic Design and Photography Degree from CUNY.

I’ve spent a great deal of my post collegiate career as a niche freelancer, working exclusively with small businesses in helping to build their brand stories along with collateral  including logos, small websites, and business cards.

Seeking a career shift and new challenge, I completed a full-stack web development fellowship program at Pursuit where I gained exposure to different coding languages through project based learning.  I also came to the realization that the UX / UI design space is where I wanted to spend time growing as an artist.

Today, I design products and experiences that are impactful and purposeful.  I am lead by the idea of always contributing to the betterment of my community, using new and cutting-edge technologies to improve people’s lives.

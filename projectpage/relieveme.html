Relieve Me

An app for Taxi and For-Hire-Vehicle drivers who are on the go and need to "go".

This web app was created within a 48hr Hackathon sprint. Future iterations included a mobile responsive design which I created after Hackathon.

Our team was awarded "Most Thoughtful" for building an app that solves a serious problem for an overlooked demographic, demonstrating thoughtfulness and care.

My Role: Lead UX / UI Designer, Logo Designer, Constructed backend tables using SQL and Express.

Frameworks & Tools Used: HTML 5, CSS3, JavaScript, Photoshop CS, PostgresSQL, Express

Wireframes

Sketching

Our goal was to make this app as simple as possible for the user. From personal experience as Uber drivers, we wanted to be able to find the closest rest stop quickly so we can park our cars without the worry of getting a parking ticket.

Colors & Typography

Staying true to the essence of New York City, it was only right that "taxi cab yellow" was incorporated in the design of this app. We also wanted a simple and legible font that's modern and clean.
